Directions to Sawmill Woods Golf Course

 
View Larger Map

From I-95:

Take I-395 Exit toward Downeast Maine
Take Exit 6-A/Rt. 1A toward Ellsworth/Bar Harbor
Travel 5 miles to stop light junction 1A and Rt. 46
Turn left onto Rt. 46
Travel 5 miles to end of Rt. 46/Junction Rt. 9
Turn right onto Rt. 9
Travel 4.5 miles to Sawmill Woods Golf Course on left

From Bangor:

Cross Penobscot River into Brewer
Turn left onto Rt. 9
Travel 15 miles to Sawmill Woods Golf Course on left

From Old Town:

Turn from Rt. 2 onto Rt. 178 toward Holden/Brewer
At end of Rt. 178, turn left onto Rt. 9
Travel 9 miles to Sawmill Woods Golf Course on left

From Ellsworth:

Take Rt. 180 from Rt. 1A
Travel to end of Rt. 180 (approx. 20 miles) to junction Rt. 9
Turn right on Rt. 9
Travel 2 miles to Sawmill Woods Golf Course on left
