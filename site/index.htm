Welcome to Sawmill Woods Golf Course

Now OPEN for the 2020 season!

Sawmill Woods Golf Course is only a 1/2 hour drive or less from Bangor, Ellsworth or Orono, so come experience the uncrowded serenity of our nine-hole par 36 course. The course was designed to follow the contours of the land and is carved from the Maine forest, so golfers are surrounded by trees and native Maine wildlife, and the large, undulating greens and tight fairways are enhanced by landscaping throughout the course. Each hole is unique, and with four sets of tees to choose from, golfers of all ability levels can enjoy a great round of play.

Experienced golfers seeking a challenge can play from our blue tees (slope rating: 124) or white tees (slope rating: 121), while less
experienced golfers or those just seeking a less challenging
round can opt for the red tees (2236 yards) or the gold tees (1551 yards).
